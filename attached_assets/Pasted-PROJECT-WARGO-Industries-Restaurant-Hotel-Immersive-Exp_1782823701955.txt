PROJECT: WARGO Industries - Restaurant & Hotel Immersive Experience

OVERVIEW:
Build an immersive restaurant/hotel website that transports users into the venue 
through video walk-throughs, interactive room tours, menu browsing, and reservation flow.
User should feel they're physically walking through the space. Google Drive database 
for reservations and inquiries.

TARGET AUDIENCE:
Diners, travelers, business guests, special occasion planners.
Focus: Luxury/boutique restaurants and hotels.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DESIGN & VISUAL REQUIREMENTS:

1. COLOR PALETTE:
   - Primary: Deep Brown (#3E2723), Warm Gold (#E6B800)
   - Secondary: Soft Beige (#E8D5C4), Charcoal (#3C3C3C)
   - Text: Dark Brown (#2C1810) on light, Beige on dark
   - Accent: Burnt Orange (#C65911) for CTAs

2. TYPOGRAPHY:
   - Headings: "Cormorant Garamond" or serif (elegant)
   - Body: "Open Sans" or "Montserrat"
   - Weights: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 600 (semi-bold)
   - Font sizes: H1 (56px), H2 (40px), H3 (24px), Body (16px)

3. BACKGROUND & TEXTURES:
   - Hero background: Full-width video (5-10 sec loop of restaurant ambiance)
   - Use subtle paper/wood texture overlays (10% opacity)
   - Smooth transitions between sections
   - Parallax scrolling on hero + key sections (subtle, max 20% offset)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CORE FEATURES:

1. HERO SECTION WITH VIDEO WALK-THROUGH:
   - Full-screen background: 15-30 second looping video
     Content: Person walking through entrance → dining area → open kitchen
   - Overlay text (centered, bold serif): "Welcome to [Restaurant Name]"
   - Animated tagline: "Where culinary art meets ambiance"
   - Scroll indicator: Animated arrow showing "Explore Below"
   - Overlay opacity: 40% dark gradient (text readable on video)

2. IMMERSIVE SPACE WALK-THROUGH:
   
   SECTION A: Main Dining Hall
   - Full-width video (10 sec): Pan shot of dining room, tables, ambiance
   - Beside video: Text description + key stats
     "Main Hall | Capacity 80 | Private events welcome"
   - Clickable hotspots on video (semi-transparent circles):
     - "Chef's Counter" → Shows 3 photos of open kitchen
     - "VIP Booth" → Shows photos + pricing
     - "Bar Area" → Highlights cocktail menu link
   
   SECTION B: Private Dining Room
   - Video + description (same layout)
   - Caption: "Intimate dinners, corporate events"
   - Lightbox toggle: "View 10 photos from events"
   
   SECTION C: Outdoor Garden/Terrace
   - Video loop + description
   - "50 guests | Al fresco dining | Perfect for celebrations"

3. INTERACTIVE 3D MENU DISPLAY:
   - Tabbed interface: "Appetizers" | "Mains" | "Desserts" | "Drinks"
   - Grid layout: Each dish = Card with image, name, description, price
   - Hover effect: Image expands, price highlights, "View Details" appears
   - Click card → Modal overlay showing:
     - Full-size image, detailed description, ingredients, calories/allergens
     - "Reserve & request this dish" CTA
   - Search functionality: "Find a dish" search bar (filters by name/ingredient)
   - Filter by: Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free

4. ROOM BOOKING/RESERVATION FORM:
   - Left side: Calendar date picker
   - Right side: Time selector (1-hour slots), Guest count dropdown
   - Additional fields: Name, Phone, Email, Special occasion checkbox
   - Optional: Special requests textarea (dietary, seating preference, etc.)
   - Button: "Reserve Table" (warm gold, hover glow effect)
   - Success state: Checkmark animation + "Reservation confirmed for [Date] at [Time]"

5. PHOTO GALLERY (Filtered Masonry):
   - 20-30 high-quality photos of restaurant/hotel
   - Filter tabs: "Dining" | "Kitchen" | "Ambiance" | "Events"
   - Lightbox on click with navigation arrows
   - Full-screen view with photo details (dish name, event type, date)

6. TESTIMONIALS VIDEO CAROUSEL:
   - 4-5 short video clips (10-15 sec each) of customers/diners
   - Speaking to camera: "Why I love this place"
   - Rotates every 5 seconds (auto-play, pause on hover)
   - Fallback text testimonials for low-bandwidth

7. CHEF'S STORY SECTION:
   - Large hero image of chef
   - Adjacent text: "Meet [Chef Name] - 20 years of culinary excellence"
   - Background video: Montage of chef cooking (5 sec loop)
   - Philosophy statement (serif font, 2-3 sentences)

8. SPECIAL OFFERS BANNER (Sticky or floating):
   - Promotion: "Book 2 weeks ahead, get 15% off group reservations"
   - Countdown timer (if time-limited): Days:Hours:Minutes
   - Dismiss button (X), never force-close

9. CONTACT & LOCATION:
   - Embedded Google Map (interactive, zoomable)
   - Address, phone, email sidebar
   - Hours of operation (expandable for special hours)
   - "Get Directions" button (opens Google Maps app on mobile)

DATABASE INTEGRATION (Google Drive):

1. RESERVATION FORM SUBMISSION:
   When user clicks "Reserve Table":
   a) Validate: Date, Time, Guests, Name, Phone, Email
   b) Check availability (read from "Availability" sheet)
   c) If available → Append to "Reservations" sheet
   d) Send confirmation email with booking details
   e) Show success modal

2. GOOGLE DRIVE SETUP:
   - Main folder: "WARGO Industries / Restaurants / [Restaurant Name]"
   - Sheet 1: "Reservations-2026"
     Columns: Timestamp | Name | Phone | Email | Date | Time | Guests | Special Requests | Status
   - Sheet 2: "Availability-Master"
     Columns: Date | Time Slot | Available Seats | Reserved Seats
   - Update availability dynamically after each reservation

3. BACKEND API ENDPOINT:
